#2 ABOUT MEHow I am?

My name is Marc Zamora, born in 1976 in Barcelona. I currently live in Mataró (BCN).

Always passionate about the visual arts and communication I decided to merge these two fields of my interest and specialize in graphic design as a form of visual communication.

In recent years I have focused my work in the online world: design, mobile apps, banner campaigns, interface design.

I’m really interested in UX techniques and methodologies, and I usually deal with Angularjs for my frontend development.

I love coffee.
